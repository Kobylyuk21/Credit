How to Build Good Credit.

To start to imporve your credit, first step would be to check what your credit is. Go online to websites like CreditKarma and check your credit score for free. Next step is to pay your bills on time so your credit score will increase. This takes time so be patient. Make sure you try to pay off any credit card balances you might have faster so your credit score will increase more. Make sure you don't close any credit cards, even if they are paid off. This will hurt your credit score and cause your score to drop. Instead once your done paying it off you, cut them up and don't request a new card. These are the best ways on increase your credit score.

  • Pay credit card bills and any other loans on time.
  • Pay off credit card balances faster.
  • Don't close any unused credit cards or open accounts.
